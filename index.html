Welcome to EcoRank!

EcoRank is an app designed to help you and your community adopt sustainable habits and make a positive impact on the environment. We believe that small changes can make a big difference!

How it works:

  • Join a Crew: After signing up, you'll be placed into a team of like-minded individuals. Each team focuses on a specific area of sustainability: Carbon Reduction, Recycling, Energy Conservation, or Water Conservation.
  • Complete Challenges: Participate in a variety of eco-friendly challenges tailored to your crew's focus. Earn points for completing each challenge.
  • Log Your Activities: Track your daily sustainable actions, such as reducing water usage, recycling diligently, or using public transportation.
  • Compete and Win: At the end of each month, the crew with the highest combined points wins exciting prizes from our sponsors!

EcoRank is about more than just winning. It's about:

  • Learning and Growing: Discover new ways to be sustainable and improve your environmental impact.
  • Connecting with Others: Meet people who share your passion for sustainability and build a community.
  • Having Fun! Engage in friendly competition and celebrate your achievements.

Ready to make a difference? Join EcoRank today!
